PRIVATE CAR INSURANCE COVERAGE
COMPREHENSIVE
Section 1.
Loss or Damage to the insured car by
(a) Accidental External means
(b) Fire, Burglary House-breaking or Theft
(c) Malicious Act.
Section 2
a. Death or bodily injury to any person excepts where such death or injury arises out of and in the course of the employment of such person by the insured and excluding liability to any person being a member of the insured's household who is passenger in such motor car - unlimited.
b. Damage to property other than property belonging to the insured or held in trust by or in the custody or control of the insured or any member of the insured's household - Rs. 10 million
Section 3.
Medical expenses incurred up to Rs. 1500/- in respect of injury sustained by insured or Occupants
COMPULSORY EXCESS
Vehicles over 10 years old excess Rs. 1,000/-
Vehicles over 15 years old excess Rs. 1,500/-
Vehicles over 20 years old excess Rs. 2,000/-
DASH CAM REBATE
A rebate of 2.5% is allowed (maximum Rs. 2500/-). If given on would be recorded from the claim, if found out the dash cam is not available or not in working condition at the time of the accident and the vedio of the dash cam (in good condition) should be provided on request by the claims department.
